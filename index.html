CAROLYN MICHAEL


Born in rural Virginia and raised on land that my ancestors purchased in the 1800’s, I did not foresee leaving my large family and living 2,100 miles from home. However, in 2002 I was given the opportunity to move to Utah and fell in love with both the state and the feeling of exploration. I have spent the time since discovering new places, friends, and interests. I returned to Virginia to complete my degree, graduating from Bridgewater College in 2009 with a bachelor’s in Business Administration and a concentration in Accounting. I worked as an accountant in the aviation field before returning to Utah in 2013. Always willing to learn new skills, I accepted a position as a Compounding Pharmacy Technician and despite being unfamiliar with pharmacy am now a lab supervisor. In my free time, I enjoy talking with friends and family, reading, camping, and trail running.
